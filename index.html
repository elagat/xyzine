Mindfulness Talks for Kids

XYZine Founder Emily Lagattolla, a former English and Mathematics teacher who has taught in schools all over the world, including Illinois and Mexico, gives talks at schools and other organizations about mindfulness, a stress-reduction technique rooted in Buddhism. Emily has been developing her meditation practice at Blue Lotus Temple in Woodstock, IL for two years. The 45-minute talk is geared towards elementary school children and entitled "Poetry, Mindfulness, and Math." It is adaptable for other audiences.
